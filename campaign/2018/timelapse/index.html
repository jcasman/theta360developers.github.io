Free Stitching and HDR App

Every person that registers for the challenge will receive a free Android application to stitch the dual-fisheye images into equirectangular and create HDR files. See rules for eligibility.

First 10 people to complete the challenge will receive a Virtual Reality Mini-Degree online course valued at $888. 237 lessons, 40 hours of video, certificate of completion.

Help Advance 360 Art

Real photographers will use the apps to create incredible art. The specifications were contributed to our community by the photographer that created these images. This is your chance to learn about 360 camera development and engage with the world of 360 photographers.
